FAQ

Questions, answered.

How is this different from traditional SEO tools?

Traditional SEO tools watch the 10 blue links. AI Rank Analysis watches what AI engines actually say in their answers — the citations, the sentiment, the recommendations. The two channels overlap less than you'd think.

Which AI engines do you track?

ChatGPT (incl. Search), Perplexity, Google Gemini, Google AI Overviews, Anthropic Claude, and Microsoft Copilot. We add new engines as they launch.

How do you generate the prompts?

We combine your category, competitors, and product features with a library of buyer-intent templates, then enrich it with prompts we observe being asked in the wild. You can add and edit prompts at any time.

Do I need to install anything on my site?

No. Tracking AI engines doesn't require a tag. Optional crawler analytics use a single line of JS or a server-side log integration.

Can I trial it before paying?

Yes. Every plan starts with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You'll have a complete dashboard before the first day is over.

How is my data handled?

We never sell prompt data. Customer dashboards are isolated, encrypted in transit and at rest. SOC 2 Type II is available on Enterprise.
